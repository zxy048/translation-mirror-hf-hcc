# Highlights

1. Translation co-expression programs are conserved across heart failure and HCC.
2. Thirty-one of 33 translation pathways exhibit mirror regulation (HCC↑, HF↓).
3. ATF4/ISR and MYC pathways converge on the translation co-expression program in HCC.
4. Cross-disease signals emerge at the pathway level, not at the single-gene level.
5. Co-expression module conservation does not confer universal prognostic value.
